Kids Choice Montessori Academy

To teach all children the importance of loving to learn and the greatness of learning to love.

Each individual is unique, provided with varied patterns of growth and development with different learning habits, skills values and attitudes.

Our Pre-School Education program provides:

  • Individualized curriculum where the child is the center of learning process.
  • Well equipped learning materials to help stimulate childs mental ability.
  • Instruction and supervision from highly qualified teaching staff.

Accordingly, KCM Academy produces OUTSTANDING STUDENTS

Some of our graduates are now honor students of big schools like De La Salle-Zobel, Colegio de San Agustin and others. Other KCMA students skip PREPARATORY level because they were accelerated to First grade. At NURSERY level, our students learn to read basic words.

Considering this principle, KIDS CHOICE offers programs and enjoyable experience for the child to:

  • Have time to find out about things and to master new skills.
  • Be more creatives, sociable and alert.
  • Have challenges that are just beyond
    their present level of accomplishment.
